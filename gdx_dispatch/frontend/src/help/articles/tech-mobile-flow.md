# Tech mobile flow

The mobile view is built for one hand on a phone, on a truck, with the engine running. Here's the day from your side.

## Morning

- Open the app on your phone. **Today's route** loads — your jobs in scheduled order with addresses, customer notes, and ETA from the previous stop.
- Tap a job to see what dispatch sent — service requested, customer notes, prior history at this address.

## Heading to a job

Tap **On my way** when you head out. The customer (if SMS is on) gets a "Your tech is on the way" message; dispatch sees your status flip.

If you need turn-by-turn, the address opens Apple Maps / Google Maps in one tap.

## On site

Tap **Arrived** when you pull up. This locks in your on-site time for payroll and lets dispatch see you're not late if the customer calls.

## Working

The job page has everything you need:

- **Customer history** — every prior job, equipment installed, warranty notes
- **Parts** — search the truck inventory or add a snapshot part if it's not in stock
- **Photos** — drag-and-drop or take photos directly; they attach to the job
- **Notes** — for the office, for the next tech, or for the customer

Lost signal? Keep working. The app stores everything locally and syncs the moment you're back online.

## Quoting on the truck

Tap **+ Estimate** to build a Good / Better / Best quote in 30 seconds:

1. Pick from your catalog.
2. The system suggests Good / Better / Best variants based on shop pricing.
3. Hand the phone to the customer. They tap, agree, and sign right there.
4. The signed estimate becomes a job and an invoice — no paperwork.

## Closing out

When the work is done, tap **Complete**. The closeout sheet captures:

- **Parts used** — drawn from your truck inventory or added as snapshot
- **Hours on site** — auto-calculated from Arrived → Complete, editable
- **Customer signature** — fingertip on the screen
- **Notes** — for the office or for next visit

Your shop may require all four before the job will close — that's a workflow gate set by the owner. Don't fight it; it keeps billing accurate so you get paid right.

## After hours

If the job has follow-up (warranty, return visit), add a note and a tag. Dispatch will see it the next morning and book the return.
