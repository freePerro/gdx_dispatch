# Welcome

DispatchApp is the operations console your shop uses to handle customers, jobs, dispatch, and invoicing in one place.

## What you can do here

- **Customers** — keep contact info, addresses, and history in one place.
- **Jobs** — schedule work, assign technicians, and track status from booked through closeout.
- **Dispatch** — see today's jobs on a board, drag to reassign, and broadcast "on the way" updates to customers.
- **Invoices** — turn completed jobs into invoices, send payment links, and reconcile against payments.

## Finding your way

- The **sidebar** on the left is your main navigation.
- The **search box** at the top jumps directly to a job, customer, or invoice — `Ctrl+K` opens it from anywhere.
- The **help button** (the question mark in the top bar) opens this drawer anytime.
- The **"Take the tour"** link in the sidebar replays your role's onboarding tour whenever you want a refresher.

Most things in the app are role-aware — if you don't see something other team members see, it's because their role has access to a different part of the system.
