# Owner — getting started

Welcome. You're now the owner of your shop's GDX console. There's no rush — this isn't a 50-step wizard. The goal of your first hour is to get a small piece of real work into the system so you can see how it flows.

## The five-thing first hour

### 1. Company info (5 minutes)

**Settings → Company.** Add your shop name (the public one — what customers see on invoices), logo, billing address, phone number, and business hours. The hours drive your booking availability later if you turn on customer self-scheduling.

### 2. Invite one teammate (5 minutes)

**Settings → Users → + Invite.** Pick the person who'll do dispatch or the office work — they'll log in alongside you and discover the flow with you. You'll both have your own logins; you can always invite the rest of the team this week.

### 3. Service catalog (10 minutes)

**Catalog.** This is your list of jobs you sell — spring replacement, opener install, tune-up, whatever's standard for your shop. We seed it with 5 common items as a starting point. Edit the prices, rename them, add yours. Each item becomes a one-click option when creating a job.

### 4. Your first customer + job (15 minutes)

Use a real customer — maybe a friend, family, or your most loyal repeat. **+ New Customer → + Job from the customer page.** Walk through the flow end-to-end: schedule it, dispatch it, "complete" it on the mobile view, generate an invoice. This is your gut-check that the workflow makes sense for your shop.

### 5. Billing (5 minutes)

**Billing → Subscription.** Confirm your plan and add a payment method. You won't be billed until trial ends. Your customers' invoices live on the same Billing page, but under a different tab — they're separate concerns sharing one screen.

## After the first hour

In your second hour:
- Import existing customers (Customers → Import → from QuickBooks or CSV)
- Connect QuickBooks Online (Settings → Integrations) so invoices flow both ways
- Invite the rest of the team — each role sees a tour for their part of the app on first login

## Where things live

- **Daily work** — Dashboard, Jobs, Dispatch
- **Money** — Billing, Payments, Reports
- **People** — Customers, Users
- **Setup** — Settings, Catalog, Integrations
- **Field** — `/mobile` for the tech view, `/dispatch` for the dispatcher view

## Help, when you need it

- The **?** button in the top bar opens this drawer with searchable articles.
- The **Take the tour** link in the sidebar replays the role-aware welcome tour anytime.
- For things in-app help can't fix, support is one form away from Settings → Support.
